Trials Fusion

Trials Fusion is a platform racing video game developed by RedLynx in collaboration with Ubisoft Shanghai and Ubisoft Ukraine.[4] A follow-up to the critically acclaimed Trials Evolution, it is the fifth game in the Trials series and is the first one to be released on a PlayStation platform. The game was released on Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, Xbox 360 and Xbox One in April 2014.[2] A companion game, Trials Frontier, released on iOS and Android devices and is designed to accompany the console and PC title.[5] Ubisoft released six paid DLC packs, as well as several free content updates.[citation needed] Online multiplayer was added in an update on January 24, 2015.

Trials Fusion was generally well received upon release. Critics praised the level design, controls, graphics, the futuristic setting, and the amount of content, but criticized the lack of tutorials for the game's create mode, and its similarity to past Trials games. By February 2015, the game had sold over 1.7 million copies.
