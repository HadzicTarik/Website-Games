American Truck Simulator

American Truck Simulator is an upcoming vehicle simulation game. It is being developed by SCS Software, and is the parallel video game to Euro Truck Simulator 2. It was revealed at the Electronic Entertainment Expo 2015 (E3 2015), although it was first announced in September 2013.[1] SCS Software plans to release the game on 3 February 2016.
