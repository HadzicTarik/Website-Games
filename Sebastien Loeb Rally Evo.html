Sebastien Loeb - Rally Evo

The game features input from nine-time World Rally Champion Sébastien Loeb, who had also won the Pikes Peak International Hill Climb and competed in the 2013 European Rallycross Championship season. Loeb had previously had some technical input with Gran Turismo 5, also appearing virtually as a mentor in that game's rally mode. The game features around 300 km of tracks, with 8 different rallies, 5 rallycross tracks and the Pikes Peak track, as well as over 50 car models.

The game does not feature content related to Loeb's involvement with the Dakar Rally, World Touring Car Championship or the 24 Hours of Le Mans.

A playable demo featuring the Rallye Sanremo was released on the PlayStation Store and the Xbox Games Store on December 24, 2015. As of New Years Day, the demo is not available on Steam.
