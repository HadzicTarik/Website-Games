Just Cause 3

Just Cause 3 is a 2015 open world action-adventure video game developed by Avalanche Studios and published by Square Enix.[2] It was announced on November 11, 2014. It is the third game in the Just Cause series and is the sequel to the 2010 video game Just Cause 2. It was released worldwide on December 1, 2015 for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One, receiving positive reviews, with critics praising the open world, destruction mechanics, and its promotion of player agency, while criticizing performance issues and the uninspired narrative.
