Dragon Quest - Heroes

Dragon Quest Heroes: The World Tree's Woe and the Blight Below[1] (ドラゴンクエストヒーローズ 闇竜と世界樹の城 Doragon Kuesto Hīrōzu Yamiryuu to Sekaiju no Shiro?, lit. "Dragon Quest Heroes: The Dark Dragon and the World Tree Castle") is a 2015 hack-and-slash game for PlayStation 3, PlayStation 4, and Microsoft Windows. It was released in Japan, Hong Kong, and Taiwan on February 26, 2015,[2][3] and in North America and Europe for the PlayStation 4 in October 2015.
