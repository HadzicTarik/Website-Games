Solarix is a 2015 Science-Fiction Stealth-based first-person shooter survival horror video game developed by Pulsetense Games and published by KISS Ltd for Windows OS. It was developed using the Unreal Development Kit and is the first video game developed by the Turkish-based Pulsetense Games. The game is intended as a homage to stealth-focused first-person video games of the late 90's and early 2000's, particularly System Shock 2 and the Thief series. It was released on April 30, 2015 on the Steam digital distribution service.[1] It has received mixed reviews.
