Triad Wars

Triad Wars is a cancelled open world online multiplayer video game developed by United Front Games in conjunction with Square Enix London Studios and published by Square Enix.[2] It is a spin-off to the 2012 video game Sleeping Dogs and the second installment in the Sleeping Dogs series. The game had more traditional multiplayer online game elements than Sleeping Dogs, with the central goal being to "rise to power as a criminal kingpin of the Triad underworld."[3] It featured the same map as the original Sleeping Dogs.
